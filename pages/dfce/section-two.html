Why Data for the Circular Economy?

Using data to monitor and manage circular economy practices is increasingly common across the globe. New initiatives led by Europe and the UNECE are establishing dimensions and indicators of the circular economy to monitor for better national policy making and decision-making, which is compelling. Yet, how these indicators are defined and used will have significant impacts on the effectiveness, efficiency and engagement of poor and marginalised people in the circular economy.
