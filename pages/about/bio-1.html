Dr. Ibukun Modupe Adesiyan

Co-Investigator; Nigeria Co-Lead

Dr. Ibukun Modupe Adesiyan is a Senior Lecturer in the Department of Biological Sciences, Achievers University, Owo, Ondo State; A fellow of the Organization for Women in Science for the Developing world (OWSD) and a member Nigerian Young Academy (NYA). She is an interdisciplinary environmental science teacher and researcher with expertise in the area of environmental impact assessment of development projects, environmental pollution source tracking and human risk assessment, water quality assessment, antimicrobial resistance surveillance and, how it impacts on sustainable and resilience resource use.
