Chukwunonye Ezeah

Co-Investigator; Nigeria Co-Lead

Dr. Chukwunonye Ezeah is a registered Civil Engineer, Chartered Environmentalist and Dean of the Faculty of Engineering and Technology at Alex Ekwueme Federal University, Ndufu-Alike, Nigeria. He graduated with a B.Eng in civil engineering and holds an MSc in Environmental Management and a PhD in Environmental Science from the University of Wolverhampton. His outstanding PhD thesis, helped inform policy decisions on waste management in Nigeria.
