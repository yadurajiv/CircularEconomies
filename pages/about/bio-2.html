Mohit Arora

Co-Investigator

Dr. Mohit Arora is a Research Associate in Sustainable Built Environment at the Institute of Infrastructure and Environment, University of Edinburgh. His circular economy research has won several recognitions including an Ernst Mach Fellowship and Train@Ed Marie Curie Fellowship. His doctoral thesis focused on developing assessment methods and applications for circular economy in cities. His research combines methods from engineering systems, industrial ecology, design science and sustainability science for delivering low carbon-built environments.
