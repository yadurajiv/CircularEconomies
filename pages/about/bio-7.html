Liberatha Kawamala

Co-Investigator; Tanzania Lead

Liberatha Kawamala is an eco warrior and creator of the social enterprise known as Libe Green Innovation. She works to promote decent jobs creation, entrepreneurship, creativity, innovation and encourage the formalisation and growth of micro-small and medium sized green enterprises. Kawamala is the Global women ambassador in manufacturing summit Canada and she is the Young Tanzania Global Leader in World Women Conference Award.
