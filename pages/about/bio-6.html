Kenisha Garnett

Co-Investigator

Dr Kenisha Garnett is a Lecturer in Decision Science. Her current research is developing robust foresight methodologies that link evidence-based strategic risk with value judgements to inform futures analyses, using outputs to communicate future challenges and opportunities to policy makers. Currently, she is the Cranfield lead implementing annual cycles of the EU foresight system for the environment (DG Environment, 2018-2023) and also the lead Cranfield supplier on the UK Government Office of Science and the Department for Business, Energy and Industrial Strategy Futures Framework research contract (2020-2024).
