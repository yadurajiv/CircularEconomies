Tasfin Aziz

Co-Investigator; Bangladesh Lead

Tasfin Aziz is an urban planner specialised in environmental planning and sustainable urban development. Currently he is working as a lecturer in the Department of Architecture, BRAC university, Bangladesh. His professional work and research activities are focusing on biodiversity conservation, climate change adaptation, urban ecology, urban resilience, and urban complexity and systems thinking. He strongly advocates for people-centered planning and implementation approach in research and professional practices.
