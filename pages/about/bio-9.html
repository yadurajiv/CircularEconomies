Abdallah Mikulu

Co-Investigator; Tanzania Co-Lead

Abdallah Mikulu is a visionary and game-changer with a bachelor's degree in Geography and Environmental Studies, has dedicated his time and energy to the principles of Circular Economy, Sustainability, and Environmental Conservation. He is a Country Representative for the World Network on Climate Reality, a project devoted to climate action and the recruitment of active climate reality leaders all over the world.
